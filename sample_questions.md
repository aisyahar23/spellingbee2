# Smart Farm Assistant - Sample Questions

Your chatbot can now answer these types of questions:

## General Farming Questions
- "Hello, what can you help me with?"
- "How do I start a garden?"
- "What are the basics of farming?"

## Crop-Specific Questions
- "How do I grow tomatoes?"
- "When should I plant tomatoes?"
- "What's the best way to care for tomatoes?"
- "How do I grow lettuce?"
- "What vegetables are easy for beginners?"

## Soil and Fertilizer Questions
- "How do I improve my soil?"
- "What's the ideal soil pH?"
- "How do I make compost?"
- "What are good organic fertilizers?"

## Pest Control Questions
- "How do I control pests naturally?"
- "What are organic pest control methods?"
- "How do I get rid of aphids?"
- "How do I prevent plant diseases?"

## Watering and Irrigation Questions
- "How often should I water my plants?"
- "What's the best time to water?"
- "How much water do plants need?"
- "How do I set up drip irrigation?"

## Organic Farming Questions
- "What is organic farming?"
- "How do I start organic gardening?"

## Seasonal Questions
- "What should I plant in spring?"
- "What crops are good for summer?"
- "How do I extend my growing season?"
- "What can I grow in winter?"

- Planting tips

## Tips for Better Responses
1. Be specific in your questions
2. Mention the crop or topic you're interested in
3. Ask about specific aspects like "planting", "care", "harvest", or "problems"
4. Use keywords like "organic", "natural", "pest", "soil", "water", etc.

Try asking any of these questions in your chatbot to see how it responds!
